9月初: 到达瑞士

  1. 办理电话卡: 在国内办理国际漫游应急一下. 可以在机场办理, 也可以安顿下来后再网点办理电话卡.
  2. 办理交通卡: 苏黎世的机场和机场火车站在一起, 在柜台上出示护照和一张护照规格的照片就可以办理交通卡, 并提供邮寄地址 (Room 302, Meierwiesenstrasse 62, 8064 Zürich)
  3. 到学校报到, 携带必要材料, 包括: 毕业证 (graduation certificate), 学位证 (bachelor degree certificate) 的原件+2份复印件; 装在中大信封5中的最终成绩单 (transcript);

  4. 办理 residence permit: 在街区办公室预约时间点到移民局办理Permit, 地址: Berninastrasse 45,8090 Zurich.

    • 在开学前两周会受到学校寄送的 extended confirmation of matriculation (ECM).
    • 带上护照/身份证, ECM, 租房合同, 20 + 65 CHF.
    • 预约采集生物数据.

  5. 使馆教育处报道: 到中国驻瑞士大使馆教育处办理报到注册手续. 访问 瑞士留学服务网

  6. 三个月内办理保险. 有问题和 International Student Support 联系. 参见 ETH 办理保险的教程. 计划购买 Academic Care 的 “CHF 110 (with an annual deductible of CHF 0), CHF 115 as from 1 Sept 20”. 申请流程
    • 在完成 Residence Permit 申请之后, 就会在家里收到一封来自 “Stadtische Gesundheitsdienste” 的邮件, 告诉我需要购买保险了. 在邮件的表格中填写信息, 在 “Name der aktuellen Krankenkasse” 的横线下填写保险公司名字 “Academic Care by Groupe Mutuel”. 然后装进 “return envelope” 中寄回原地址.
    • 打开 www.groupemutuel.ch/eth , 仔细阅读并选择保险类型, 跟着网站的指示进行操作.
    • 下载并打印 表格1, 以及 表格2. 填写好之后把它们寄回 Groupe Mutuel 的网站 (邮寄或电子邮件). 同时把 Residence Permit 以及 COnfirmation of Matriculation 同时也寄过去.
    • 等待保险卡寄到家里
    • 等待 KVG 豁免通知.
  7. 办理银行卡: 拿到 presidence permit 之后, 办理 UBS 的信用卡和储蓄卡. 注意, 将来搬家时, 及时去银行更换地址!!

时间与气候条件

瑞士的气候是属于温带海洋性气候, 全年温和潮湿, 冬无严寒，夏无酷暑，一年四季降水比较均匀. 在天气网摘取了瑞士的全年平均温度与降水天数

**瑞士**



瑞士位于东 1 区

与之相对比的是广州的气候: 大致上来说, 每个月苏黎世的气温都比广州低10-15摄氏度. 但降雨天数并不会有显著的差别. 但广州的年降水量达到1600mm, 而苏黎世约为广州的一半, 只有700mm

**广州**



广州位于东 8 区

另外, 再来看一看旧金山的气候. 真是在国内待久了, 都不知道国外的气候是怎么样的. 旧金山的年平均降水量是广州的五分之一, 十分干燥. 此外, 年气温变化非常少, 最高温和最低温的年平均极差都不到10摄氏度. 夏天最低平均气温居然和冬天最高平均气温一样???

Proseminar & Seminar Project

During the MSc degree programme, students have to conduct a proseminar or a semester project involving research on a theoretical or experimental topic appropriate to the students’ level. After completion the MSc Thesis can be started.

In a proseminar students do a guided self-​study of original papers and of advanced textbooks in physics. Within the general topic, determined each semester, participants give a presentation on a particular subject and deliver a written report.
In semester projects physics students who focus in theoretical physics have to conduct a small theoretical semester research project, on a topic appropriate to the students’ advanced level. Students with a focus on experimental physics conduct a project in a research laboratory (min. 3 weeks full time lab work, plus time for preparation, writing a report and giving a presentation, totally ca. 210 working hours).

Semester projects are preferably carried out at one of the institutes of the Department of Physics, but may also be conducted at a research institution (for example, PSI or CERN) or in industry. In the category Proseminars and Semester Projects in the Course Catalogue, further courses are offered that are equivalent to a semester project. Semester projects can be carried out any time, in consultation with the supervisor.

Five step to a successful semester project

  1. Step 1: Look for a suitable theme and a professor to supervise you (see the helpful tips below). If you do not want to carry out the semester project at the Department of Physics, look for a professor in the department before you begin the project who is willing to recognise the selected topic as an experimental semester project. Then enrol in myStudies (category Proseminars and Semester Projects).
  2. Step 2: Carry out the project and write the report. The written report is compulsory and cannot be substituted by an extension of the lab work.
  3. Step 3: Ask your supervisor for corrections to and feedback on your report and the work you have done.
  4. Step 4: Present your scientific results in a public talk. In consultation with the supervisor this talk should be conducted in the research group where you conducted the semester project.
  5. Step 5: The supervising professor awards you the ECTS credits. Please note that the report has to include a Declaration of Originality. (PDF, 622 KB)
